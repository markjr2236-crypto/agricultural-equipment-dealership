# Agricultural Equipment Dealership

A Clarity smart contract designed to manage agricultural machinery sales with comprehensive inventory tracking and service coordination. Built for equipment dealers who need to streamline their operations on the blockchain.

## Core Features

The contract handles essential dealership functions through Clarity maps that track equipment inventory, dealer profiles, and service requests. Equipment management includes make/model details, pricing, and availability status, while the service scheduling system connects customers with dealers for maintenance needs.

## Implementation Notes

Leverages Clarity's principal validation for secure dealer registration and uses data variables for auto-incrementing IDs. The contract owner controls dealer registration, ensuring quality control while individual dealers manage their own equipment listings. Service requests are automatically assigned and tracked through completion.

This approach provides a solid foundation for agricultural equipment trading with built-in service management capabilities.
